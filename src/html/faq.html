Delivery

  • What delivery methods do
    you use?

    United Kingdom: We offer Royal Mail 48 hour Tracked delivery service FREE with all orders above £18. We also offer the option to upgrade to Royal Mail 24 hour Tracked service- simply select this option at the checkout United Kingdom: We offer Royal Mail 48 hour Tracked delivery service FREE with all orders above £18. We also offer the option to upgrade to Royal Mail 24 hour Tracked service- simply select this option at the checkout

  • What is your dispatch
    time?

    We endeavour to dispatch all orders placed before 12pm the same day, please note this is not guaranteed. After 12pm, all orders placed will be dispatched the next working day (Monday-Friday).

    my address?

    my order?

    If your order has not yet been dispatched, you can cancel your order within free of charge. You will be notified via email of your parcel’s dispatch. Once your order has been dispatched and your order is in transit...

  • Can you ship to other
    countries?

    If you would like to ship to an address outside of the UK/ROI please get in touch with our support team and we can discuss possible shipping options that will best fit your needs. We will always do our best to ship to ...

  • How can I
    contact you?

    If you need to get in touch, please reach out to our customer support team by email at support@pulsio.co.uk, or on our social media platforms for the fastest response time. We aim to reply to all customer queries within 24 hours.

  • Where are you
    located?

    We are a UK based company and our team is based in our offices in London. All our stock is dispatched from our hubs in Bristol and Kettering. This includes orders to Ireland/Europe.

  • Ireland Deliveries and
    Customs/Border charges

    We have recently launched a new dispatch hub in Galway, Ireland to provide fast and free delivery to our customer base in ROI, with shipping options to Europe and beyond also provided.

    my parcel?
